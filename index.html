    About PP Top Rank Services Limited

    We have extensive experience and are ready to meet most of the requirements of the commercial shipping and cruise industry.

    We are a professional chandling company who are reliable and capable in providing chandling services to vessels. We offer a wide range of fresh, frozen and dry provisions to serve diverse preferences and needs of our customers, while still adhering to the demands of present food supply chain and international foods standards.

    We are unique and have distinguised ourselves as one of the leading chandling company with a standing reputation for excellence and top notch innovation that provides and end to end supply services to our clients.

    As much as the company has grown, we remain committed to the following principles;

    • Quality: Provide Top notch products and services to our clients.
    • Integrity: We keep to our words as honesty is key.
    • Reliability: Provide good quality that our clients can depend on.
    • Respect: Team members respect everyone regardless of Gender, Educational background, Race, Skin Color, Religion, Lifestyle.Everyone is treated equally wihout discrimination.

    We aim to be a top ranking and most reliable ship chandler in Africa while delivering quality products at competitive price in line with customers expectations and satisfaction.
